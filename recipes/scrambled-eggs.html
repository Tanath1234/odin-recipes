Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Want to know how to make an exquisite, fluffy, delicous golden scrambled eggs that melts in your mouth? Well here's a very simple recipe that can be done just under 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tbs of Chicken Seasoning Powder
  • 1 tbs of Butter
  • 1 gram of Chopped Chives

Instructions

  1. Beat 3 eggs in a small bowl until fully mixed
  2. Add the chicken seasoning powder and stir again
  3. Place a pan on medium to high heat and drop in the butter
  4. Once the butter starts to sizzle, place in your eggs and stir generously
  5. Take the pan on and off the stove with a interval of 10 seconds so you dont over cook them
  6. Once the eggs are 80% cooked, turn of the stove and place your eggs in your plate!
